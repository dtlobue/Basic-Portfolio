About Me

Hello Internet wanderer, my name is Daniel Truitt Lo Bue and I am a newcomer to Los Angeles and web development. Growing up, I split time between living in New York City with my father and New Jersey with my mother. Having thrown my family's money away attaining a liberal arts degree, I decided to re-work my life plan and pivot towards software development.

My interests include a wide range of music (from hardcore to ambient to alt-country), physical exercise (I am a licensed personal trainer), reading and writing, comic books (talk to me about "Stray Bullets"), martial arts (wannabe jujitsu head), the Balkans, the Russian language, film (Carpenter, Kurosawa, Kobayashi, Cronenberg, and other weirdos), and even more.
